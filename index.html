About Me

Back in 2016 I obtained my BAS in Exercise Science from the University of Minnesota Duluth. After graduation, I worked in cardiac rehabilitation for 3 years, but ultimately ended up deciding that healthcare wasn’t the path for me. I did some soul searching, and decided to try out this thing we call “coding”. I’ve always been really into puzzles and problem solving, so it didn’t take long for me to realize I have a knack for software development. That’s when I discovered Prime Digital Academy, a full time, 21 week, immersive program for people like me who wanted to make a career switch and get into tech!

Outside of coding, my passions include running, exploring new restaurants and breweries, and going on trips to the Boundary Waters.

PupperManagement

PupperManagement is a mobile first web application to aid in the management and tracking of responsibilities involved in dog ownership. Features of the app include keeping track of meals, walks, and other outside time. This app especially caters to people who share responsibility in taking care of a dog, (ie: couples, roommates, families, etc).

Lady Boss League by Find Your Power

Currently working with Find Your Power, a nonprofit based in Minneapolis that focuses on expanding resourses to groups of underrepresented women. Lady Boss League will be a network and referral web app for women to use to connect each other with other women/women-owned businesses.
